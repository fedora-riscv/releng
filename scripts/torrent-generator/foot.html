To use this system do the following:

  • Download bittorrent:
    Use Fedora Extras: yum install bittorrent
    or
    from the homesite
  • install bittorrent
  • Open up ports 6881-6999/tcp so other clients can contact you for bits
  • figure out where you want the isos to go on your system
  • download one of the torrent files above:
    wget http://torrent.fedoraproject.org/torrents/$torrent-filename-from-above
  • To download run: bittorrent-curses --max_upload_rate 350 $torrent-filename-from-above-here.
  • Once your download is complete please leave your downloader running so it can help upload to the other clients. This is what makes bittorrent efficient.
